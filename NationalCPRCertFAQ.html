What if I didn't receive an email to create my RQI account?

Please check your junk/spam folder in your email account for an email from donotreply@rqi1stop.com. After checking, if you did not receive an email, please contact NationalCPRCert support.

How can I access a copy of my certification?

After completing an assignment, you can view/print your card at any time by launching the assignment and clicking the View eCard button. Once you view your eCard, you can also use the QR code included to save a copy to your phone. You may also use the Email eCard button to send a copy to your email address.

​

Is RQI approved by the American Heart Association (AHA)?

Yes. RQI provides the AHA’s highest credential, the RQI eCredential, which certifies competency. Learners will also be issued a two-year AHA completion/compliance card which may be provided to other institutions as needed that do not utilize RQI.

How will I be notified if I have an assignment to complete?​

You will receive an email from RQI Partners and RQI1Stop (​donotreply@rqi1stop.com) when an assignment has been added. It will provide information on what assignment has been made, as well as how to access RQI.

Do I need to complete an RQI assignment if I already have a valid two-year certification?

Yes. RQI is your institution's approved solution for maintaining your certification once your facility is live with the program.

Return to top

​

How often must I complete my assignment?

Assignments must be completed once per calendar quarter after completing your initial assignment.

Return to top

​

Can I complete the RQI quarterly activities in advance?

No, new assignments are only made on a quarterly basis and are unavailable until the first day of the quarter.

Return to top

​

What should I do if I am overdue to complete a quarterly assignment?

Overdue quarterly assignments must be completed in full before the current assignment will be available. After completing the current assignment, you will be compliant.

Return to top

​

How much/what type of CME/CE is awarded for RQI?

After successful completion of four quarterly RQI assignments, staff have the option to claim CME/CE credit. Individual credentialing organizations have the sole discretion over whether offered credit is accepted.

Credit that m​ay be claimed at the completion of all four quarters:

  • 0.75 credits - RQI Provider
  • 1.75 credits - RQI ALS and RQI PALS

How do I claim CME/CE credit?

After completing four quarters of an assignment, you may claim your CME/CE at any time by launching the assignment and clicking the Claim CME/CE button. Fill in the form, and click Save. Then click the Download Certificate link next to the Select CME/CE list at the top of the Claim CME/CE Credit page.

​ What is the cleaning protocol?

Please only use an alcohol-based cleaning wipe/solution. Please do not use bleach.

The best practice is to wipe down the station/mannequins before and after completing skills.

​What do students struggle most with at the station so I can best coach those needing help?

Correct technique for BVM is the most common struggle at the station. Emphasize the E-C technique where the little, ring, and middle fingers form an “E” and rest on the mandible and provide chin lift, whereas the index finger and the thumb form a “C” around the mask to provide a tight fit of facemask over patient's face.

​​​

Inability to get enough depth on the chest compression is likely due to not using the step stool.

View this tips sheet with other helpful information: RQI Tip Sheet​

What resources are available to support learners who need skill remediation?

There are several resources available:

  • The videos within the skills portion of the course provide a quick and focused review of key elements of the skills.
  • Remember the 100 beats per minute. Consider having a playlist of songs available on your phone while helping to coach someone needing to improve his/her compression.
  • Learners can refer to the learner manuals, which are available online inside each eLearning course from the menu in the online exam.
  • Contact an RQI Champion in your facility for more assistance with skills.
